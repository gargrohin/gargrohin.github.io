Biography

I am a senior year undergraduate at IIT Kanpur, majoring in Electrical Engineering. I am broadly interested in Artificial Intelligence, Algorithm Design, Robotics and System design and their application in real world problems. I am currently working with Prof. Vipul Arora in Music Information Retrieval, and am a part of institute IGVC team. I have previously interned at Adobe BEL labs where I worked with Reinforcement Learning and User Behaviour modelling.

Apart from my academic pursuits, I enjoy creating and covering music through all sorts of instruments, playing football for fun and lawn tennis for school.
